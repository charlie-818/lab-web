Featured Project: Sharmo

Sharmo is a revolutionary blockchain platform transforming how people invest in real estate through asset tokenization.

At Sharmo, we're breaking down barriers to real estate investment by enabling fractional ownership through blockchain technology. Our platform allows users to:

  • Invest in real estate with as little as $100
  • Trade property tokens securely on our purpose-built marketplace
  • Access comprehensive property analytics and investment metrics
  • Earn passive income through automated rent distributions
  • Build a diversified property portfolio without the traditional barriers

The platform features an intuitive React-based interface with secure KYC verification, blockchain transaction management via Web3.js, and ERC-721 token implementation for true asset ownership.
